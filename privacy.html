Privacy

A J Tyzack and Co. Ltd collects information about clients via this web site (www.ajtyzack.com). The collection, storage and usage of this information is detailed below.

Customer information collection and usage

Any information given to A J Tyzack and Co. Ltd is treated with care and stored securely. We use industry standard technology to keep submitted information secure. Our procedures safeguard and secure the information we collect online, however, no internet data transmission is guaranteed 100% secure. As a result we cannot warrant the security of any information transmitted to us via this web site. You do so at your own risk. Once we receive your information, we make our best efforts to ensure its security on our systems.

By using this site, you agree we may collect and use information only for the purposes for which the information was collected, and in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not distribute personally identifying information on or via this web site.

How your information may be shared

A J Tyzack and Co. Ltd will never sell or rent your personal information. We will only provide your information to other persons, if:

  • We have your consent.
  • We provide the information to our suppliers or agents who assist us in serving you and they have agreed to abide by our privacy policy.
  • We are required to do so by law.

What is "Personally Identifying information"?

“Personally identifying information” is information that can be explicitly used to identify and/or contact you as an individual. This includes information such as: email address, first and last name, occupation, company name and postal code / zip code. Other information collected includes any information relating to internal company based promotions.

Personally identifying information that you choose to provide to another party or web site will be subject to their privacy policy. For example, if you purchase goods or services online from a merchant you found while visiting the A J Tyzack and Co. Ltd web site, information you provide to that merchant is subject to their privacy policy rather than ours.

A J Tyzack and Co. Ltd may ask you for personal information such as a postal address. We do this when you register for services that require such information. This information may also be needed for purposes of compliance with federal, provincial and local laws.

Links to other sites

Users should be aware that when you are on the A J Tyzack and Co. Ltd web site, for example, in the links page, you could be directed to other sites beyond our control. There are links to other sites from the A J Tyzack and Co. Ltd web site that take you outside of our service. These other sites may send their own cookies to users, collect data or solicit personal information.

The A J Tyzack and Co. Ltd policy does not extend to anything inherent in the operation of the internet and therefore beyond A J Tyzack and Co. Ltd control. It is not to be applied in any manner contrary to applicable law or governmental regulation.

Sending Email to A J Tyzack & Co Ltd

When you send us email we will use your name and email address only when responding to the concerns raised in the email. This means your email may be shared with the appropriate people within the A J Tyzack and Co. Ltd network to help us respond to your email.
